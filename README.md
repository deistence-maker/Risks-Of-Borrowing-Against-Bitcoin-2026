Understand the risks of borrowing against Bitcoin in 2026. Learn about liquidation, volatility, platform risks, and how to protect your crypto assets.

---

## Introduction

Bitcoin loans offer flexibility—but they come with real risks.

---

## Major Risks

### Liquidation Risk
BTC drops → collateral sold  

### Volatility Risk
Crypto markets are unpredictable  

### Platform Risk
Custodial failures  

### Interest Costs
Loans must be repaid  

### Over-Leverage
Borrowing too much increases exposure  

---

## Risk Mitigation

- Use low LTV  
- Monitor positions  
- Choose secure platforms
